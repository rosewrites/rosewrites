Morning Pages: Clear Your Mind Before Work#

Morning Pages are 3 pages of freewriting done first thing in the morning.

Why They Work#

  • Clears mental clutter
  • Sparks creativity
  • Reduces anxiety

How to Do Them#

  1. Grab a notebook.
  2. Write 3 pages of whatever comes to mind.
  3. Don’t worry about grammar or structure.

Tips#

  • Do it immediately after waking up.
  • Keep your notebook ready at your desk or nightstand.
  • Review once a week for insights.

“Writing your thoughts clears space for action.”
